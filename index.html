    Service

    At Bliss salon, we would like to offer you the best services in the most relaxing environment. We care for your health by using single-used or extremely sanitized tools. All of our beauty products are brand-name or certified organic. By limiting on doing acrylic nails, we make sure there is no heavy chemical odor or harmful dust in our salon air.

    Manicure

    Including basic manicure, gel manicure, dipping powder and acrylic powder.

    Pedicure

    Including basic pedicure, Milk & Honey pedicure, the Sport (for men)/the Work (for women) pedicure, Deluxe Pedicure, VIP pedicure and Pure Zen Pedicure .

    Waxing

    Including eyebrow wax, lip wax, chin wax, side burns wax, face wax, chest wax (for men), back wax (for men), bikini wax and brazilian.

    Eye lashes Extension

    Including individual style, hybrid style , volumn style, and cluster

    Massage

    Including Escape Massage 30'(focus to head, neck and shoulder), Retreat massage 50', Pure Zen Massage 70'.

    Facial

    Including basic skin care, Retreat package and Organic package.
